The Lords of Easy Money: How the Federal Reserve Broke the American Economy

by Christopher Leonard

Published: 2023

Pages: 384 pages

Theme: Economics & Money

The Lords of Easy Money reveals how the Federal Reserve’s unchecked policies—particularly money printing and ultra-low interest rates—have fueled economic bubbles, rising inequality, and financial instability. Investigative journalist Christopher Leonard takes readers inside the Fed’s secretive world, exposing how a small group of policymakers has shaped the modern economy in ways that benefit the wealthy while leaving the middle class behind. With a gripping narrative and insider accounts, this book unveils the true cost of “easy money” and why the system feels rigged. A reminder that bad money makes for bad times—ignore at your own peril.

Author Bio

Christopher Leonard is an investigative journalist and bestselling author known for his deep dives into economic and corporate power structures. His work focuses on the hidden forces shaping financial markets, from banking institutions to monopolistic corporations. In The Lords of Easy Money, Leonard exposes the Federal Reserve’s pivotal role in widening economic inequality and market instability. His previous books include Kochland, a groundbreaking exposé on corporate influence in American politics and policy.
